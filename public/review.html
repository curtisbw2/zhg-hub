Substack poster (bookmarklet)

Substack blocks posting from servers, so Substack halves post through your own browser. Setup (once): press Ctrl+Shift+B to show the bookmarks bar, then drag the button below onto it.

Use: after clicking Post now on a note, a Substack tab opens automatically. Click the bookmark there and a panel appears listing every note awaiting Substack. Tick the ones you want and post them.
